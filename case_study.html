Transforming Challenges into Quantifiable Success for Humanity Impact Institute Org

The Challenge: When Ads Go Wrong

So here's what was happening with the Humanity Impact Institute's Google Ads - it was kind of like trying to sell ice cream to people who are lactose intolerant. Their ads were showing up for all the wrong searches, getting clicks from people who weren't even in the right country, and basically burning through money without getting any real results. The worst part? They were tracking the wrong thing entirely - instead of counting people who actually signed up for internships (which was their real goal), they were counting donations. Imagine trying to measure how many people liked your cooking by counting how many said "nice weather today" - that's basically what was happening. They needed someone to come in and fix this mess so their ads actually worked for them instead of against them.

The Fix: Making Every Dollar Count

Here's the cool part - I came in and basically gave their Google Ads a complete makeover. First, I did a full audit to see what was broken (spoiler alert: almost everything). Then I restructured their whole campaign, cleaned up their keywords by getting rid of the bad ones and adding better ones, and even adjusted their bids based on what devices people were using. But the biggest win? I discovered their tracking was completely broken - they were counting donations instead of internship applications! So I built them a proper tracking system from scratch that actually measured what they wanted. The results were pretty sweet - instead of wasting money on random clicks, they started getting real internship sign-ups.
