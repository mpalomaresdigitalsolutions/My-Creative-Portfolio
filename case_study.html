Google Ads Campaign Optimization: A Case Study

Transforming Challenges into Quantifiable Success for Humanity Impact Institute Org

The Challenge: Wasted Spend and Irrelevant Traffic

Humanity Impact Institute Org, a non-profit dedicated to global education initiatives, initially faced significant challenges with their Google Ads campaigns. The primary issue stemmed from default Google Ads settings that led to inefficient spending. Specifically, the use of broad keyword targeting meant ads were triggered for a wide variety of searches, many of which were irrelevant to their mission. Furthermore, the default "Presence or interest" location targeting, while seemingly helpful, was attracting clicks from users merely interested in their service area, not those physically located within it. This combination resulted in a high volume of clicks from unqualified leads outside their target demographic, leading to wasted ad spend and a high effective cost per conversion. They needed a strategic partner to refine their digital outreach, ensuring every advertising dollar contributed directly to high-quality engagement and support for their mission.

The Result: Amplified Mission, Sustainable Growth

My strategic optimization efforts led to a remarkable transformation in Humanity Impact Institute Org's Google Ads performance. While the raw numbers for clicks and conversions might appear to have decreased, this was a *deliberate and positive outcome*. By meticulously refining location and keyword targeting, I successfully eliminated a significant volume of irrelevant, low-quality traffic that was previously consuming budget without contributing to the non-profit's mission.
